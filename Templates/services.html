Cardio

Elevate Your Stamina, New York! Ignite Your Cardiovascular Fitness at Our Top-Notch Gym for Unstoppable Energy and Vitality!
Cardio exercise, which is sometimes referred to as aerobic exercise, is any rhythmic activity that raises your heart rate into your target heart rate zone. This is the zone where you burn the most fat and calories. Cardio” comes from the Greek word “kardía,” which means heart. Simply put, exercise typically counts as cardio if it involves intentional coordinated physical actions that raise your heart rate for an extended period of time.
